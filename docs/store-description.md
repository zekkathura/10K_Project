## Full Description (4000 characters max)

10K Scorekeeper is the ultimate companion app for the classic dice game Ten Thousand (also known as 10K, Farkle, or Greed).

Stop using paper scoresheets! Track scores digitally with quick-add buttons, invite friends with game codes, and see your game history and statistics.

**Features:**
• Quick-add buttons for common scores
• Create games with 2-10 players
• Real-time score updates across devices
• Track your wins, best scores, and stats
• Dark and light themes
• Add guest players who don't have accounts
• Free to use - no ads, no premium tier

First to 10,000 wins! Download now and never lose track of scores again.
