Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

10K Scorekeeper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Display name - Your chosen name shown to other players in games
  • Password - Securely hashed, never stored in plain text

Game Data

  • Game scores - Points recorded during gameplay
  • Game history - Records of games you've played
  • Player statistics - Aggregated stats like wins, average scores

Authentication Data

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and name from the respective service. We do not receive or store your Google or Apple password.

Apple Sign In: If you choose to hide your email when signing in with Apple, we receive a private relay email address that forwards to your real email.

Error and Diagnostic Data

To improve app stability, we automatically collect:

  • Error messages - When the app encounters an error (no personal data included)
  • App version - Which version of the app you're using
  • Platform - Android, iOS, or web
  • Screen context - Which screen the error occurred on

This data is sanitized to remove any personal information (emails, IDs, etc.) before being stored. Error logs are only accessible to developers and are used solely to identify and fix bugs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable multiplayer game functionality
  • To display your scores and statistics
  • To allow other players to identify you in shared games
  • To diagnose and fix app errors (using anonymized error data)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a secure cloud database platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Row-level security policies
  • Regular security updates

Tracking and Analytics

10K Scorekeeper does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not:

  • Use advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • Share data with advertising networks
  • Track your activity outside of our app
  • Build profiles for targeted advertising

The only analytics we collect are anonymized error logs to fix bugs (see "Error and Diagnostic Data" above).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Other players - Your display name and game scores are visible to other players in games you join
  • Service providers - We use Supabase for data storage, and Google/Apple for authentication
  • Legal requirements - If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data associated with your account
  • Update - Change your display name and other profile information
  • Delete - Delete your account and associated data through the app settings

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is permanently deleted
  • Your game history is anonymized (scores remain for other players' records, but are no longer linked to you)

Error logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes, then automatically deleted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
